Algo-trading

Algorithmic trading refers to the use of algorithms to make better trade decisions. Usually, traders build mathematical models that monitor business news and trade activities in real-time to detect any factors that can force security prices to rise or fall. There are many kinds of trading algorithms but mostly my focus is on Signal algorithms. Unfortunately, it is forbidden to trade signal algorithms directly in the Tehran stocks exchange, but still, we can try to enhance trading strategies using signals in a non-automated trading system.
