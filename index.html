About Me

I'm Sajad Faghfoor Maghrebi, a second-year PhD student in the SysNet group at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Niv Dayan at ORCA Lab. My research focuses on scalable vector indexes, a cornerstone for semantic retrieval in AI, and designing data structures optimized for modern hardware infrastructures.

I'm particularly interested in creating efficient database systems and indexing techniques that minimize data access costs while scaling. My work bridges theory and practice, which I find especially rewarding.
